<objective>
Fix the Metal layer zombie bug that causes a visible artifact when closing the app window with Cmd+W.

Purpose: The ghostty surface is freed while the Metal CALayer is still attached to the window's contentView hierarchy, causing the Metal layer to persist as a zombie. Ghostty's own codebase solves this with a specific teardown order: (1) contentView = nil to detach Metal layer, (2) Task.detached surface_free to free asynchronously on MainActor.

Output: Three modified Swift files implementing correct ghostty surface lifecycle teardown.
</objective>

func destroySurface() {
